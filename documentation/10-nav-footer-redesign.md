# Step 10: Redesigning Navigation & Footer (Cyborg Theme)

The Navigation and Footer components have been updated to serve as the "Mainframe Interface" and "System Protocol" layers of the portfolio.

## Actions Taken

1.  **Navigation Enhancement**:
    - Updated the navbar to a "Tactical Hub" aesthetic with glassmorphic depth and neon-cyan accents.
    - Replaced the standard logo with a high-contrast "LJ" system identifier.
    - Added `hover:glitch` effects to navigation links.
2.  **Footer Transformation**:
    - Re-titled the contact section to "Initialize Connection" for a technical call-to-action.
    - Styled the footer with "System Information" metadata (e.g., "© PROTOCOL_2026").
    - Updated social links with tactical glowing backgrounds and rotational animations.
3.  **Visual Consistency**:
    - Ensured background gradients and blur effects match the `cyber-grid` theme established in previous steps.

## Design Goal

To provide a cohesive frame for the entire website that reinforces the Cyborg/Futuristic theme from top to bottom.
