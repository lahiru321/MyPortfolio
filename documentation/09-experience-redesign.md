# Step 9: Redesigning the Experience Section (Cyborg Theme)

The Experience section has been transformed into an "Operational Deployment Log," framing professional history as a series of critical system missions.

## Actions Taken

1.  **Thematic Rebranding**: Re-labeled "Experience" as a "Deployment History" log.
2.  **Structural Updates**:
    - Replaced standard timeline dots with pulsing "Node Reactors."
    - Applied a glowing, data-pulse effect to the timeline vertical axis.
3.  **Visual Elements**:
    - Styled cards as "Mission Files" with technical metadata (e.g., "Status: COMPLETED").
    - Used monospace typography for dates and technical summaries to enhance the "system terminal" aesthetic.
4.  **Interactive Glows**: Added cyan and neon-blue glow effects on hover to highlight specific "deployments."

## Design Goal

To present the user's career path as a sequence of successful technological deployments, emphasizing reliability and technical depth.
